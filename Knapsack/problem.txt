Suppose that you have a knapsack problem.
You are given N items of weights 𝑤1, 𝑤2,… 𝑤n and their values are 𝑣1, 𝑣2, …, 𝑣n, respectively.
The knapsack has capacity 𝑊. Your goal is to find the most valuable subset of the items that fit into the knapsack.
You can pick from each item as many as you can.
